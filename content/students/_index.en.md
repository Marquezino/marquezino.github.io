Detailed information regarding procedures, requirements, and deadlines can be found on the official COPPE/PESC admissions page. Admissions occur on a quarterly basis, and prospective applicants are encouraged to pay close attention to deadlines.

Undergraduate students interested in research activities, including Scientific Initiation projects, are welcome to contact me directly.

International students should check [https://internacional.ufrj.br/en/international-relations/].

Researchers interested in postdoctoral opportunities are encouraged to get in touch to discuss potential projects and funding possibilities. Collaborations aligned with my research themes are always welcome.
